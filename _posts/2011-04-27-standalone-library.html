--- layout: post title: Standalone Library wordpress_id: 93 date: 2011-04-27 15:26:46.000000000 -07:00 tags: - news --- A little history and an update: after Rich Kilmer integrated my graphics library into Apple's MacRuby project as part of the "HotCocoa" library, the HotCocoa project was split out from MacRuby into its own gem at github.com/richkilmer/hotcocoa. Then, Matt Aimonetti extracted the graphics library out of HotCocoa into its OWN standalone library, MacRuby Graphics. Here's my fork of that project, where future development efforts will be directed: github.com/drtoast/macruby_graphics
